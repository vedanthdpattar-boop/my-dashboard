🌊 RescueGrid AI – Intelligent Flood Disaster Management Platform

📌 Overview

RescueGrid AI is an AI-powered disaster response and flood relief management platform designed to help governments, NGOs, rescue teams, volunteers, and citizens coordinate efficiently during flood emergencies.

The platform provides a centralized command center for monitoring affected areas, managing relief operations, allocating resources, tracking rescue teams, collecting donations, and generating AI-driven disaster insights.

By combining real-time analytics, intelligent resource allocation, volunteer coordination, and predictive AI models, RescueGrid AI enables faster decision-making, minimizes aid delays, and improves disaster response effectiveness.

🚀 Problem Statement

During floods, relief efforts often face:

Lack of real-time visibility
Poor coordination between agencies
Uneven resource distribution
Duplicate aid delivery
Delayed emergency response
Limited volunteer management
Inadequate disaster analytics

RescueGrid AI addresses these challenges through a unified digital disaster management platform.
